How is Finolity different from multiple clubs or technical panels in every college?

a. Frequently organizing talks, lectures, interaction with young, successful Entrepreneurs. This young entrepreneur share their journey from student to entrepreneur. Basically objective of such activities is to inspire and motivate students to take up entrepreneurship over job.
b. Organizing focused workshops on Design Thinking, Idea Generation, Business Model Canvas, Business Plan Pitch-deck from resource person who has vast experience in entrepreneurial teaching.
c. Organizing Competition such as Idea competition, Business Plan competitions etc. with theme start-up and entrepreneurship. Idea and B-plan competition can also be sector specific instead of in general for example; FinTech, Food Processing, Social Innovation etc.
d. Organizing outreach activities with outside organization such as TiE and nearby Incubation center.
e. Organizing National level event. Almost every reputed e-Cell has their annual flagship event.
f. Applying for Government Entrepreneurship Scheme. To support Entrepreneurship government have various Start-up and Entrepreneurship scheme at different level.
